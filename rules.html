Welcome to Digital Rights Monopoly!

Welcome to a world where digital rights take the centre stage! In this game, you will navigate different scenarios, where you can learn and advocate for digital rights.

Objective

Your goal is simple: support digital rights issues and accumulate the most influence and money by the end of the game to win.

Getting Started

  • Number of Players: Form a group of 2 to 6 players for a varied and engaging game experience.
  • Setting Players: Before beginning, select the number of players. If you are playing solo or wish for a challenge, you can set a player as a computer
  • Player Details: Begin by entering your name or a nickname and choose from a selection of pre-made avatars.
  • Starting Funds: You will start with 2500 DIGRA currency units to buy properties and manage other expenses.
  • Who Goes First: Let the game take the lead! Watch as it shuffles the rolls and announces the first player to kick things off.

How to Play

  • Roll the dice: On your turn, roll the dice to advance your avatar along the board.
  • Unowned Space: If you land on an unowned space, a pop-up will explain its associated digital rights issue, giving you the option to buy or auction it.
  • Owned Space: Landing on a space owned by another player will prompt you to pay the required fee.
  • Chance or Community: Find yourself in one of these spaces? Draw a card and follow the instructions.
  • Zero Rating: Landing here means you will skip your next turn, a pop-up will provide more information on the "Zero Rating" concept.
  • Jail: In jail, you won't collect D200 units on your next turn. To leave, roll matching numbers on both dice or pay a fine. After three failed exit attempts, paying the fine is compulsory.

Important Points

  • Board Spaces: Each board space represents a different digital rights issue. Owning them helps you to increase your influence.
  • Team Up: Players can form alliances, trade properties, and strategize together to enhance their chances of winning.
  • Guidance: If in doubt, refer to the on-screen prompts and hints that are available to guide you.

Play wisely, learn, and strive for victory while promoting a safer and inclusive digital world. Good luck!
