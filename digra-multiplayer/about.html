ABOUT THE GAME



Digital Rights Monopoly started as a board game in 2018. It's designed for learning as much as playing. The game is based on real stories from young people in the Digital Grassroots community, which spans over 40 countries. It helps players explore the complex digital world.


Our game was created to address the unequal power dynamics in today's digital landscape. Often, the narrative is controlled by a few, leaving out marginalized voices. This game aims to change that by providing a space for youth with limited digital access to learn, share, and face digital challenges.


In 2023, we took the game online with support from C/Change's 2nd Creative Research & Development Lab. Our open-source platform promotes a decentralized view of the digital world, focusing on inclusivity and fairness. Each move in the game helps you understand digital rights and Internet Governance better, encouraging you to shape your own digital future.
