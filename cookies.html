Cookie Policy



Last updated: 13 February 2024

Welcome to Digital Grassroots Monopoly! This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies on our website to enhance your gaming experience. Please read this policy carefully to understand what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.



What are cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of data stored on your browser when you visit a website. In the context of Digital Grassroots Monopoly, cookies are used to store game data locally on your device, allowing you to recover your game progress and preferences.



How we use cookies

Digital Grassroots Monopoly does not store cookies on any of its servers. Cookies are only stored on your browser to help play the game. Digital Rights Monopoly uses cookies for the following purposes:

Game progress and Preferences: We store game data locally on your browser to enable you to resume your game from where you left off.

Preferences: Cookies are used to remember your preferences, such as sound settings or language preferences.



Types of cookies we use

Persistent cookies: These cookies remain on your device even after you close your browser and are used to store your game progress and preferences.

Session cookies: Session cookies are temporary and are deleted from your device once you close your browser. These cookies are used for temporary storage, such as remembering your session ID while you play the game.



Your choices regarding cookies

You have the option to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of Digital Grassroots Monopoly, such as saving your game progress and preferences.
